Tigers are more than just beautiful creatures; they are symbols of strength, grace, and ecological importance. Discover their world, their struggles, and how we can help.

Why Tigers Matter

  • Being apex predators, they regulate the populations of their prey to stop them from destroying the ecosystem. (1)
  • Tigers hold a deep cultural significance in many communities in Asia.
  • Protecting tigers also leads to preserving ecosystems that house many other amazing creatures.
  • Tiger-based ecotourism generates a lot of revenue for local communities, incentivizing conservation efforts over exploitation. (1)
  • The fight to save tigers has catalyzed international cooperation, creating frameworks and technologies that benefit broader conservation efforts worldwide.
